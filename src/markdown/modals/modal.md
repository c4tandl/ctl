The Center for Teaching and Learning will hold its spring Open House on Sunday, April 12, from 3:30-5:00. 

All members of the midcoast community are invited to tour the school, meet the faculty, and learn about the program. Families with children of all ages are welcome to attend and ask questions about the school. 

For the 2026-27 school year, CTL currently has limited openings in pre-k, kindergarten, second grade, and fifth grade.

We hope you will join us!

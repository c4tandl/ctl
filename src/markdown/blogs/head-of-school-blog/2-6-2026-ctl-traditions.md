Hello, CTL Families,

Traditions are important markers in schools - markers of joy, of hard work, of pride, of community building, just to name a few. At CTL, we love our traditions! Two of our most beloved CTL traditions are happening in February. Winter Carnival will be held next Friday, February 13, from 8:45-12:00. CTL will celebrate Lunar New Year with the 3-4 Dragon Dance and schoolwide feast on Wednesday, February 25. Lunar New Year will be coming up quickly after February break, so mark your calendars now. We hope you will join us in the fun! Thank you for your support and contributions of time and food. 

Winter Carnival:

Thank you to everyone who has signed up for both Winter Carnival activities and snacks! We still have some openings so here is the activities sign-up genius with some of the planned activities and room for you to write in your own, and here is the snack sign up. Please remember to not bring anything containing peanuts or made with peanut products for all-school events.

Valentines: The afternoon of February 13 will be our class Valentine's celebrations. Students can make valentines at Winter Carnival, of course, but many choose to prepare at home. Thanks for supporting your kids for Valentines! 

We will once again include a Collections Fair in this year’s carnival. If your child collects anything—we’ve had bottle caps, marbles, comics, rocks, key chains, Pez dispensers, sea shells, refrigerator magnets, wooden boxes, and more—the Collections Fair is a chance to display something that they have amassed. Each collector takes a few minutes first thing on the morning of the carnival to lay out the collection. Then everyone else takes time during the morning to stop by and appreciate the items on display. Every time we’ve done this, it has been great to see what kids, and even teachers and staff, bring in.

Lunar New Year:

We're so excited to celebrate Lunar New Year and the Year of the Horse on Wednesday, February 25, with a special Morning Meeting with a Dragon Dance performed by the 3-4 class. 

At noon, we'll hold a Lunar New Year feast - always a CTL favorite for the students. Please use the signup genius, linked here, to choose a contribution for the feast, and thank you in advance. When making food for Lunar New Year or any all-school event, please keep everything peanut-free.

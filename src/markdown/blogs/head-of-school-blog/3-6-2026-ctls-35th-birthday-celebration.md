Hello, CTL Families,

This 2025-26 school year marks 35 years since the Center for Teaching and Learning first opened its doors on Cross Point Road. We’re thrilled to be celebrating CTL’s 35th birthday this year and reflecting on the past, present, and future impact of the school. 



We want to invite you, our beloved current families, to be part of this event that will also include alumni, alumni families, our Board, and current and past faculty and staff. We will join to celebrate CTL on Saturday, May 16, from 4 to 6pm—save the date!



Please see more information and RSVP at our Evite invitation. At this link, we also encourage you to contribute to the lead-up to the event: by leaving a written birthday message for CTL, by recording a video message, by adding pictures to one of our shared Google photo albums spanning CTL’s eras, or by purchasing special 35th gear.



Your RSVP will help us to plan for the event, which will include opening up the school as a “museum” of the school’s traditions, with interactive displays in various rooms, and a short speaking program along with food, drink, and connection down in the Barn. There will not be dedicated childcare at the event, so we ask that any children who attend please remain with their caregivers.



If you’re available and interested in helping with set-up or other aspects of the event planning, please contact me at anne@c-t-l.org 



We’re excited to see so many folks who have been impacted by CTL at different points in the school’s history, and we hope that you—the present family community that makes CTL so vibrant and strong—will be able to join us for this special day as we celebrate this milestone and look toward the future of teaching and learning at this place that means so much to us all.

Love, 

Anne
